THINK WHOLE SOLUTION, NOT JUST SOFTWARE

Often great ideas arise, but with no takers. Often great products are made, but with no adoption. There have been some truly spectacular products that have also failed, spectacularly.
Why? Because their entire ecosystem isn’t considered.
Swachh Bharat movement doesn’t end with just building toilets. It works only when people embrace the solution and start using it. Similarly, building a piece of software is not the entire solution. To have a product be truly successful, its entire ecosystem needs to be considered.

Thus our theme for Converge Bangalore 2016 - ‘Think whole solution, not just software’. Come be part of our movement to learn, network, and share about how the tech industry is not just about software but something beyond.

Hope to see you on Saturday, 26th November.
