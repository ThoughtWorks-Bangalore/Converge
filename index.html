Responding to Disruptive Change

Is Digital Disruption Destroying the Retail Industry or Fostering it?

Track 1

"Offline First" - Do you always need internet for your application to work?
by Ranganathan Balashanmugam (App Dev, ThoughtWorks) & Venkata Padmavathi (App Dev, ThoughtWorks)


We live in connected world -- till the internet connections can be flakey and non-existent. That should not disturb the user experience. "No internet connection" is a network problem and not the application problem. Let’s hear the importance of "offline first applications" and the technologies that enable that to happen.

Track 2

The Future Bank
by Amit Tare (Project Manager, ThoughtWorks)


While on one hand the technological advancements and rising socio-economic status of vast populations is forcing the banks to change the way they operate, the rise of disruptors from within the fin-services space like "Fintech organizations” etc and from outside the fin-services space like Apple Pay etc is completely going to change how the "future bank" would look like. Let’s hear how the future bank will look like.

4:45 PM

Gamillionaire - How multiplayer gaming has taken the market by storm
by Akhil Sahdev (App Dev, ThoughtWorks)


The popular multiplayer game Dota 2 has developed into a multi-million dollar industry in less than a span of five years. What was the genius behind it? What was the business model that drove it to evolve into a mammoth force that it is today?
